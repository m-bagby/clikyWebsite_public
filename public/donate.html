Tip the Creator

Cliky is a passion project created by an independent developer to bring a more responsive experience to computer users. It is proudly brought to you completely free of charge. Hopefully you've been enjoying the unique feel it provides.

If you would like to show your appreciation for the hard work that went into its creation, your support would be gratefully accepted. By donating, you will help enable the continued growth and development that can better improve cliky, and bring more one-of-a-kind applications like it to users.

Thank you for considering a donation. Every contribution helps in creation and improvement.
